WHAT'S IOT


IoT stands for "Internet of Things" and refers to the concept of connecting physical devices and objects to the internet and to each other .This allows us to collect and share data, communicate with other devices and systems, and perform automated tasks based on that data.


IOT The New Trend


IoT has the potential to revolutionize industries such as healthcare, transportation, and manufacturing by enabling new business models and creating new opportunities for innovation. The widespread availability of inexpensive sensors, processors, and connectivity technologies such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth have made it easier and more cost-effective to connect devices to the internet. This has led to a proliferation of smart devices such as home appliances, wearable technology, and industrial machinery that can be remotely monitored and controlled.
